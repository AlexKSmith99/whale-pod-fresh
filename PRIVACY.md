# Privacy Policy for Whale Pod

**Last Updated: November 14, 2025**

## Overview

Whale Pod ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Name
- Profile picture (optional)
- Password (encrypted)

### Notion Integration
When you connect your Notion account:
- We store an access token to sync your tasks
- We create and access databases in your Notion workspace
- We read and write task information (titles, descriptions, status, assignments)
- We do NOT access any Notion pages or databases you don't explicitly share with Whale Pod

### Usage Data
- Pod memberships
- Messages and communications within the app
- Team board tasks and updates
- Profile information you choose to share

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the Whale Pod service
- Sync tasks between Whale Pod and your Notion workspace
- Enable collaboration with other pod members
- Improve and optimize our application
- Communicate with you about updates or issues

## Data Storage

- Your data is stored securely using Supabase (our backend provider)
- Notion access tokens are encrypted and stored securely
- We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties
- Your Notion data remains in your Notion workspace under your control

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Disconnect Notion integration at any time
- Request data export

## Notion Integration

- Whale Pod uses the Notion API to sync task data
- You can revoke access at any time via Notion's integration settings
- Disconnecting Notion will not delete your Whale Pod data
- Your Notion workspace data remains under your control

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication using Supabase Auth
- Regular security updates and monitoring

## Children's Privacy

Whale Pod is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- GitHub: https://github.com/AlexKSmith99/whale-pod-fresh/issues

## Third-Party Services

Whale Pod uses the following third-party services:
- **Supabase**: Backend, authentication, and database
- **Notion**: Task synchronization (when you connect your account)
- **Agora**: Video calling functionality (when enabled)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your data.

## Data Retention

- Active account data is retained while your account is active
- Deleted accounts and associated data are permanently removed within 30 days
- Notion integration data is only stored while connected

## Your Consent

By using Whale Pod, you consent to this Privacy Policy and our collection and use of information as described.
